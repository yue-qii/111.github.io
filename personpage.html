I am currently a PhD student in Villanova University under the supervision of Dr. Mojtaba Vaezi. My research topic is physical layer security in NOMA. I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in electrical engineering from Xidian University, China, in 2015 and 2018, respectively, under the supervision of Dr. Hongbing Ji, and Dr. Mingzhe Zhu, and Co-advisor Dr. Fa-Long Luo

Research Interests

  • Wireless Communications

  • Signal Processing

  • Non-Orthogonal Multiple Access (NOMA)

  • Physical Layer Security

  • MIMO Beamforming

  • Time-frequency Analysis

  • Signal Classification and Identification
